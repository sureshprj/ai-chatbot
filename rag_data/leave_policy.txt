LEAVE POLICY
============

1. Leave Categories
-------------------
a. **Casual Leave (CL)** – 10 days per calendar year  
b. **Sick Leave (SL)** – 10 days per calendar year  
c. **Earned Leave (EL)** – 15 days per calendar year (credited monthly)  
d. **Optional Holidays (OH)** – 2 days per calendar year (as per employee preference)  
e. **Maternity Leave** – 26 weeks (as per Maternity Benefit Act)  
f. **Paternity Leave** – 10 days  
g. **Bereavement Leave** – 3 days  
h. **Compensatory Off (Comp-Off)** – Must be availed within 30 days of approval.

2. Leave Application Process
----------------------------
- All leaves must be applied through the official HR portal or email to the manager at least **2 days in advance** (except emergencies).
- Sick leave beyond 2 consecutive days requires a **medical certificate**.
- Leave is considered **unapproved** until the manager approves it in the system.

3. Leave Encashment
-------------------
- Only Earned Leave (EL) can be carried forward or encashed at the end of the year.
- Maximum carry-forward limit: **30 days**.
- Casual and Sick Leaves will **lapse** at year-end if unutilized.

4. Loss of Pay (LOP)
--------------------
- Absence without approved leave will be treated as **Loss of Pay**.
- Three consecutive days of unreported absence may lead to disciplinary action.

5. Public Holidays and Weekly Offs
----------------------------------
- Public holidays are as per the published holiday calendar.
- Saturday and Sunday are weekly offs unless project requirements dictate otherwise.

6. Emergency Situations
-----------------------
- Employees are encouraged to inform their manager via phone or message if sudden illness or emergencies prevent timely leave application.

7. Unplanned Leave
------------------
- Frequent unplanned leaves may affect performance evaluations.
- HR and managers may request a discussion if absenteeism becomes excessive.

8. Joining/Resignation Cases
----------------------------
- Leave balance will be calculated up to the last working day.
- Any excess leave taken will be adjusted from final settlement.

9. Non-Compliance
-----------------
- Falsifying medical certificates or leave reasons may result in disciplinary action up to termination.
